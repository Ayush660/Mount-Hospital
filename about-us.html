About us

Mount Multispeciality Hospital is an integrated health system, including Speciality clinics and a Multispeciality physicians and surgeons group practice. MMSH is led by Individuals who bring a wealth of Knowledge to the utmost care of patients. The Board of Trustees is composed of a dedicated group of Individuals who work tirelessly to the care of the patients in making a healthier population in our region and beyond. At MMSH we care for a healthy people both in and out of Hospital. Because everyone has to live his life to the fullest. This is a system where care is based on value, not volume. Imagine a health system that rewards quality , not quantity. Imagine a health system where patients when well informed receive only the care they want and need.

Mount Super Specialty Hospital is run by specialists who have more than a decade of experience in their respective fields. The hospital has 24x7 services including Out-patients, In-patients and Emergency Care. The various specialties like General Medicine, General Surgery,Orthopedicsand Gynecologyare available throughout the day. Super specialty consultation like Vascular Surgery, Urology, Neurosurgery, Surgical Oncology, Cardiology, Nephrology, Medical Gastroenterology services will be available on appointment basis.

The hospital has both private and semi-private rooms.

The operation theatre is full-fledged with c-arm machines and angiogram compatible operation table.

MMSH partners with Mount Clinics who share our commitment to population health, creating innovative methods and models of care that provide the highest quality and best results at a fair cost to our patients.

MMSH Deparment of Vascular Surgery provides the highest level of innovative, compassionate care for our patients and contribute to solving the problems of the patients through new treatment, prevention and community engagement.

The mission is to improve the heath - locally and regionally .we do this by educating the public and creating awareness among the population regarding the illeffects of the leading problem of the community like Diabetes, Heart and Kidney diseases. We regulary conduct camps to screen the people for Diabetes and impart an awareness among the population about Diabetic and Kidney diseases.
